## App name

GoMode

## Short description

Pick a mood, discover nearby ideas, and turn “what should we do?” into a plan.

## Long description

GoMode helps you move from indecision to a practical next step. Choose the kind
of outing you want—such as a date night, family activity, quick food stop, local
adventure, or road-trip break—and browse focused suggestions without juggling a
stack of searches.

Build a simple plan, explore nearby places, save ideas for later, and keep useful
stops together. GoMode can use your location while the app is open, or you can
browse from a supported default city. When live services are unavailable, the
app clearly labels demo or fallback content so suggestions are not presented as
live facts.

GoMode is a planning companion, not a guarantee. Always verify opening hours,
prices, availability, accessibility, venue policies, and route conditions with
the provider. Do not interact with the app while driving.

## Feature bullets

- Mood-based discovery flows for everyday plans and spontaneous outings
- Date-night and road-trip planning with focused place suggestions
- Nearby browsing with optional foreground location access
- Saved ideas and collections for future plans
- Transparent live, unavailable, and demo/fallback states
- Responsive, accessible design with reduced-motion support

## Privacy summary

GoMode requests location only while you use the app and supports browsing from a
default city when location access is unavailable or declined. Search intent,
selected locations, and route inputs may be sent to GoMode's protected backend
and the service providers needed to return results. Local preferences, saved
ideas, and bounded response-cache data may be stored on the device.

The current MVP does not sell personal data. Advertising, premium, sponsored,
and lead-capture features are disabled by default and must not be described as
active unless the shipped build, consent flow, policy disclosures, and store
forms support that claim. Final store privacy forms must be completed from the
actual production SDK and data-flow inventory.
